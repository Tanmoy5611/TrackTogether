CO2 calculation model

How my savings are calculated

Every joined travel group is compared with a baseline where you would have travelled alone by car.

  • [[#{html.car}]]: 0.120 kg CO2/km per person
  • [[#{html.carpool}]]: 0.120 kg CO2/km divided across passengers
  • [[#{html.public.transport}]]: 0.040 kg CO2/km per person
  • [[#{html.bike}]]/walk: 0 kg CO2/km
  • Fallback distance: 10 km when no activity distance is set
  • Personal CO2 savings = personal baseline emissions - personal actual emissions
